About Us

Our ride is a low speed electric vehicle modeled after a 1930s roadster owned and operated by Randy and Carol Briley. The car is battery powered and we recharge our batteries with the North Myrtle Beach sunshine! Not quite a taxi, but not a limousine; not a shuttle, nor a tour bus; we've created our own category to get you around town in style.

We also offer private tours

From your pick-up point in Windy Hill down Ocean Boulevard to the intracoastal waterway and Barefoot Landing, you'll get an overview and history of the area while you enjoy a ride in a stylish, fun, and Green car.

All tours are $40.00 for up to four people in your party

Tour times are 9, 10, and 11 a.m. - Call to book now!
